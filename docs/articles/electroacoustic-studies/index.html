Noise Wrangler

Sonic Explorations by Jean-Philippe Drecourt

Electroacoustic Studies

A collection of three short electroacoustic pieces written under the excellent guidance of George Alveskog. The composition was inspired by Annette Vande Gorme's excellent Traité d'écriture sur support.
