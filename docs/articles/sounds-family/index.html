Sounds for/from my family

This is the recording of a sound performance for my extended family. To make it more involving, I asked the various members of the family to send me their own samples. I received a really eclectic collection of everyday life recordings which I blended using Chaos Conductor, my electroacoustic improvisation instrument written in Max/MSP.

    Clarinet ensemble tuning
  • Metal whisk
  • Toaster
  • Thermomix startup
  • Electric blinds
  • Construction vehicle beeps
  • Toddler vocalisations (my personal favourite, kept for the end of the performance)
  • Rainfall
  • Excavator engine start

  • Medicine bag flapping
  • Coffee machine

  • My own snores (not recorded by me :/)
