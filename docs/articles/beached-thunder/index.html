Noise Wrangler

Sonic Explorations by Jean-Philippe Drecourt

Beached Thunder

Electroacoustic and noise improvised album.

The simple recording of waves in Skagen, Denmark, gently caressing the shore becomes the foundation of this experimental music project. This album embarks on an improvisational odyssey that delves deep into the realms of sound and feedback. It's an exploration crafted using an early version of Chaos Conductor, my electroacoustic improvisation instrument, blending the organic with the electronic.

Through two intense improvisations, the album explores aspects of noise and feedback in a manner inspired by Iannis Xenakis' avant-garde compositions. It immerses listeners in a landscape where the hum of electrical guitar merges with the rhythmic lapping of waves. This juxtaposition creates a raw soundscape that invites you to experience sound in its most unfiltered form.
