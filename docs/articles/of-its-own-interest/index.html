Noise Wrangler

Sonic Explorations by Jean-Philippe Drecourt

Of its own Interest

80s electronic music inspired album.

Inspired by the early 80s electronic music of Jean-Michel Jarre and jazz rhythms and harmonies, sound artist Jean-Philippe Drecourt's foray into rhythm ranges from chillout and smooth melodies to angry and unstable beats.
