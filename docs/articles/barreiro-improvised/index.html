Barreiro Improvised

Barreiro Improvised is a series of live improvised performances mixing the sounds of Barreiro with voice, sound objects and drumming.

The project seeks to reframe how we listen to the urban and natural soundscapes and to offer a fresh perspective on the familiar sounds of the city. The project won the OUT.RA Local Creation Grant 2024.

Final performance at AMAC

Joined by Bruno Afonso on drums and Sara Zita Correia on drums, voice and sound objects, this concert is the result of a week long residency at the AMAC Auditorium in Barreiro.
