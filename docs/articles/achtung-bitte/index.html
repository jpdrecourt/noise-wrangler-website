Noise Wrangler

Sonic Explorations by Jean-Philippe Drecourt

Achtung Bitte

Live electroacoustic improvisation with airport PA call.

Live electroacoustic improvisation full of noise and feedback using Chaos Conductor, a complex Max/MSP patcher for electroacoustic improvisation based on a field recording of a public announcement in Frankfurt Airport.

The video is created offline with Jitter and heavily inspired by one of Matteo Marson's excellent Max/MSP tutorials.
