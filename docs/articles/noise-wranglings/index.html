Live electroacoustic improvisations

Live electroacoustic improvisations based on field recordings and the Chaos Conductor.

A collection of live electroacoustic imporisations based on various field recordings and using my custom sampler programmed in Max/MSP.

A Desert Storm

Featuring singing frogs at dusk in the river below the Pont du Gard in France.

Achtung Bitte

Noise and feedback based on a field recording of a public announcement in Frankfurt Airport.

The video is created offline with Jitter and heavily inspired by one of Matteo Marson's excellent Max/MSP tutorials.

Waves and other sounds

The samples used in this improvisation are:

  • a creaky microwave door
  • some waves on the Tagus river
  • An electronic sound created with SuperCollider
  • Handmade copper bells
