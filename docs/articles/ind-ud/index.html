Ind Ud

This piece features the unique voices from Copenhagen's transport system checkpoints, organized into a polyrhythm with some gentle, random delays for texture. The grounding rumble is an unintentional gift from the initial moments of the recording when handling the microphone.

My friend G infused the project with his unique, shadowy touch through a montage of fuzzy, close-up photographs of Denmark's public transport system.
