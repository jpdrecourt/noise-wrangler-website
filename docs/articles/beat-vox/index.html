Sonic Explorations by Jean-Philippe Drecourt

Beat Vox

Electroacoustic track inspired by the inability to understand a foreign language.

Beat Vox is inspired by the feeling of living in a foreign country and not understanding the language. One perceives the words as sounds devoid of meaning but full of structure and rhythm. By repeating small phrases, I'm attempting to strip the words from their meaning and reveal the musicality behind them.

Piece created under the guidance of Åke Parmerud during his masterclass at Lisboa Incomum in 2019.
